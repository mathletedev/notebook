## When are loops needed?

- No -> No loop required
- Yes -> Do you know in advance how many steps are repeated?
	- No -> Use a conditional loop
	- Yes -> Use a counting loop

```mermaid
flowchart TD

C --> D(Add score to sum)
D --> E(Increment score count)
E --> B
B ---->|Yes| F(Calculate average)
F --> G(Print average)
```
