> Conditional statements rely Boolean *conditions*, represented by a `0` or `1`

0 = false
1 = true

## Rational operators

- `<` less than
- `>` greater than
- `<=` less than or equal to
- `>=` greater than or equal to
- `==` equal to
- `!=` not equal to

## Logical operators

- `&&` AND
- `||` OR
- `!` NOT
