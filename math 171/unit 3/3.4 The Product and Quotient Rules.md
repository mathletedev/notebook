## Product Rule

$$ \frac{d}{dx} (f(x)g(x)) = f(x)g'(x) + g(x)f'(x) $$
$$ (fg)' = fg' + gf' $$

## Quotient Rule

$$ \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} \right) = \frac{g(x)f'(x) - f(x)g'(x)}{(g(x))^2} $$
$$ \left( \frac{N}{D} \right)' = \frac{DN' - ND'}{D^2} $$
