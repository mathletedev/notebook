> A way to represent a position on a circle

$$ \Delta \theta = \theta - \theta _0 $$

Different from linear position, which uses meters, angular position uses *radians*

$$ \frac{s}{r} = \theta $$

s: linear distance
r: radius of circle
theta: angular position

$$ \theta \cdot r = s $$
