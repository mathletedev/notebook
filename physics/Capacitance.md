> [[Charge]] stored between parallel plates

Capacitance is measured in *Farads* (F)

$$ C = \kappa \epsilon_0 \frac{A}{d} $$
$\epsilon_0$ = $8.85 \times 10^{12}$

$$ U_c = \frac{1}{2} QV $$

## Capacitors

### Parallel
$$ C_p = \sum_i C_i $$

### Series
$$ \frac{1}{C_s} = \sum_i \frac{1}{C_i} $$
